Particle Collision
Between particles.

Collisions between particles and objects have been studied and used. Parts that require large amounts of computation, such as collisions between particles and particles, are sometimes omitted in real-time rendering environments, such as games.

Image-based
Collision Detection Texture.

Currently, commercial game engines use a lot of methods to process collision between particles and objects through depth buffer. The method of projecting a three-dimensional image onto a two-dimensional image was adopted using the approach through Image, which is an advantage of this method.

OpenGL

The research project was implemented using OpenGL. The project goes through a two-step algorithm. The first step is to process the collision and update the particle position, speed, direction, etc. The second step is responsible for rendering the screen.
